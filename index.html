Flock Watch

A heads-up detector for automated license-plate readers. It pulls live camera locations from OpenStreetMap and alerts you when one is near — or coming up ahead.

01
Live data. Every surveillance:type=ALPR node is fetched fresh from the Overpass API as you move. Confirmed Flock units show in violet; other ALPRs in amber.
02
On-device only. Your location never leaves your phone except as an anonymous radius query to OpenStreetMap. No account, no tracking, no server of mine.
03
Alerts ahead. Sound, vibration, and a banner when a camera enters your alert range or sits in your direction of travel.

Use as a passenger or mounted hands-free — never tap while driving. Crowdsourced data is incomplete: an empty map does not mean no cameras are present. Map data © OpenStreetMap contributors / DeFlock.
